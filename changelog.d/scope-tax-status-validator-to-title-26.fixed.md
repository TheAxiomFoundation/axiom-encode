Scope the tax-status component validator to U.S. Title 26 (Internal Revenue Code) sources only. Other titles use overlapping vocabulary like "surviving spouse" in unrelated legal senses — 7 USC 2012(j) (SNAP "elderly or disabled member") references "person is surviving spouse of veteran with specified Title 38 disability status" as a demographic predicate, not a tax-filing-status component. Without this scope, encoding 7 USC 2012(j) deferred the per-member rule and broke downstream SNAP imports.
