`axiom-encode check-source-staleness --rulespec-root X --corpus-path Y` verifies the RuleSpec toolchain's named, signed local corpus release, recomputes `module.source_verification.source_sha256` pins, and reports modules whose release-bound source text changed or disappeared. `axiom_encode.source_hash` exposes `resolved_source_verification_block` and `provenance_block` for the encoder pipeline.
