The `migration-inventory` command is the reproducible source for the corpus-path census across RuleSpec checkouts: it records the scanned-module total, checkout commits, release-selector digest, classified resolution outcomes, and scan completeness. The separate historical-manifest census is produced by `manifest-census` together with `scripts/generate_v1_rollout_inventory.py`; the frozen v1 rollout inventory is decrement-only after rollout and is not regenerated as part of the corpus-path census.
